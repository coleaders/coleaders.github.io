What is Vow Renewal?

バウリニューアルとは

01

A vow is not something you make once.
It's something you renew every year.

Vow Renewal is a ritual we practice once a year, around our wedding anniversary. The word "vow" means a solemn promise — and this practice is about renewing that promise intentionally, year after year.

Inspired by the concept of "Myth Shift" from ORSC (Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching), Vow Renewal is a practice we have done for over 10 years as a couple. A relationship doesn't stay the same — it evolves. And so should the promises you make to each other.

  • * ORSC® is a registered trademark of CRR Global.
Step 01

Reflect

Look back on the past year together — not just what happened, but how you each felt. Sharing emotions alongside events opens up conversations that might otherwise stay unspoken.

Step 02

Dialogue

Talk about how you want your relationship to continue. This isn't a problem-solving session — it's a space to rediscover what truly matters to each of you.

Step 03

Ritualize

Give your reflection and dialogue a tangible form. Write letters, take photos, visit a meaningful place — the ritual is yours to design together.

Is This for You?

こんなペアに

02

  • You want your anniversary to mean more

    If your anniversary feels like it passes the same way each year, Vow Renewal offers a deeper dimension — a moment to truly check in with each other, not just celebrate.

  • Being together has become "just normal"

    Not a bad relationship — just routine. That feeling is actually a sign of stability. Vow Renewal transforms that stability into a moment of conscious renewal and fresh connection.

  • You want to update, not reset

    For couples who don't want to go back to the beginning, but forward — together. Vow Renewal honors everything you've built and helps you grow from it.

Our 10th Anniversary

10周年の記録

03

February 22, 2024 Kamogawa Delta, Kyoto

For our 10th anniversary, we decided to make the Vow Renewal truly special. We chose the Kamogawa Delta in Kyoto — a place we've visited together many times over the years. We gathered 10 years' worth of photos and laid them out, one year at a time, revisiting what happened and how we each felt.

"I didn't know you felt that way back then."
"There was so much I never got to say."

A photographer joined us as we read letters we'd written to each other. We laughed. We cried. We sat in silence and just listened. Everything that had happened across those 10 years had shaped who we are as a couple — and we could finally see it, together.

Vow Renewal, for us, has never been about going back to the beginning. It's about seeing how far we've come — and choosing to keep going, together.

Session Flow

セッションの流れ

04
01

Initial Interview

After you apply, we start with a 30–45 minute conversation. We'll talk about where you are as a couple, your anniversary timeline, and what kind of experience you're hoping for.

02

Session Design

Based on the interview, we design a Vow Renewal experience just for the two of you — how you'll reflect, what you'll explore together, and how you'll ritualize it. Fully tailored.

03

Vow Renewal Session

A 2–3 hour session facilitated by our two coaches, Junji & Shiho. We guide you through reflection, dialogue, and ritual — online or in person, whichever works for you.

04

Follow-up

Within two weeks of your session, we offer a 30-minute follow-up to check in on how you're doing and explore any insights that have continued to emerge.
